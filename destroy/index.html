Destroy Everything Game

It's the year 2030 A.D. Because of global warming, the oceans have warmed so much, the heat has awakened ancient creatures frozen for millions of years. The cities by the sea are at constant danger, both from the rising water and angry monsters that come out of it. These monsters are bigger and scarier than anything that has ever been seen before.

Destroy Everything!!!! is a multi-player cooperative game, with two game play options, a monster vs soldiers option as well as, all players on a team of soldiers defending the cities against the monsters. The soldiers get to drive tanks, fly drones, planes, and helicopters. They will use guns, rockets, railguns, grenades, and mines to defend the cities. The player who was the best in defending the city gets to choose to play the monster as a reward.

The game is set in a very dynamic fully destructible environment. You can shoot and damage anything you see, walk into every building, and explore what's inside. If you want to exit you can just blast your way through a wall of your choice.
